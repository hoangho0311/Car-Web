EXTERIOR

The new generation Ghost has been released since September 2020, after 10 years the first generation appeared on the market. The long-wheelbase (EWB) version was introduced shortly thereafter, in response to customer demand and to complement the Rolls-Royce product range. Ghost is oriented towards younger customers, while brother Phantom usually caters to middle-aged customers. In the new generation, Ghost has a new look, adding more soundproofing materials to create the quietest cabin.

FURNITURE

The interior compartment stands out in Mandarin orange and black, dotted with Polished Walnut Burr veneer on the dashboard and dashboard. The steering wheel integrates many hands-free buttons, the gear lever is integrated behind the steering wheel. Ghost 2021 uses BMW's iDrive system with an infotainment screen and digital multi-information dashboard, Wifi, USB, USB-C, HDMI, AV, Blu-ray connectivity.

In front of the rear seats, there are two picnic tables and two entertainment screens, which can be opened / closed via the side push button. When the door is closed, the interior compartment generates a "Ghost" frequency, at this frequency, occupants can whisper when the car moves at 120 km / h and is isolated from outside sounds. In essence, this is a technology that generates an inverse wave frequency to cancel the noise wave.

Rolls-Royce Ghost EWB 2021 uses a 6.75 liter twin-turbo V12 engine, with a capacity of 563 hp and 850 Nm of torque, integrated with a special port inside the air intake to reduce unwanted noise. Power is transmitted through AWD drive system and 8-speed ZF gearbox.
